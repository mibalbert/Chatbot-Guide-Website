Features

You can view your personal timetable by either asking complex questions such as What is my personal timetable for this week? or by simply typing week

You can have quick access to PDF's such as Show me the EEC Handbook or Give me the Harry Poter Sorcerer's stone book"

You can view your upcoming assignments by typing Show me all of my assignments or simply assignments

You can set up 1-2-1 meetings with your supervisor by typing I want to set up a 1-2-1 meeting with my supervisor or simply 1-2-1

You can view your grades in a Bar Chart style by typing Show me all of my grades or simply grades

You can access the core details relating your course, such as EEC Handbook or Programme Specification, just ask!

There are many other features such as Chit-Chat. You can ask What is the weather like? or Tell me a joke!
